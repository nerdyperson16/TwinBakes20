My name is Kenneth Rivera Bustamante,
I enjoyed making food for my boys and tried making some No-Bake Cheesecake. At first, I made a No-Bake Blueberry Cheesecake and I even shared it to my nieces and nephews. Afterwards, I made pastas and more.
I even made No-Bake cheesecakes like Mixed Berries, Ferrero Cheesecake, Mango Cheesecake, Oreo Cheesecake with Walnuts, and Oreo Cheesecake with Nutella.
Last September 2018 as a banker, I decided to resign and focus on my twin boys. I continued to bake using my only toaster and I tried making other treats like homemade pretzel, Keto Peanut Butter Cookies, Choco-Chip Cookies, Brownies, and a lot more.
On October 2018, I bought a convection oven. I tried making homemade Pizza from scratch.
One morning, I remember enjoying to eat homemade Banana Bread from my friend. And I started searching for a recipe so I can make it for my own. On December 2018, I made the perfect Banana Bread and shared it a friend and she loved it. I even tried exploring something new for her Banana Bread recipe. Remembering that she had family members who are Diabetic, I researched for an ingredient to make her Banana Bread safe for older people, health conscious, and diabetic.
I started making Banana Bread for my family and some friends. Sometimes, I visited my work and shared my Banana Bread and everyone loved it.
